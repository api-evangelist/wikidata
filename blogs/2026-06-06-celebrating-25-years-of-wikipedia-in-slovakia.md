The Wikimedians of Slovakia celebrated Wikipedia's 25th anniversary with a community gathering at Bratislava City Library that drew 15 attendees. The event featured presentations on Wikipedia's history, a quiz competition, a photo exhibition from previous community drives, and celebratory cakes. Organizers hope to see larger participation when Slovak Wikipedia specifically reaches its 25-year milestone in 2028.
