WikiEval is a newly renamed web-based platform, formerly called WikiContest Tool, that streamlines Wikimedia contest management by consolidating submission tracking, evaluation, and scoring into one centralized location. The platform offers centralized contest management, supports multiple scoring systems, and reduces manual administrative work for organizers, participants, and jury members. Community feedback is actively being sought to improve the tool's features and usability.
