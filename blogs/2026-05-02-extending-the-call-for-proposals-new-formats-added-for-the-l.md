<p>We’re excited to share an update on the <strong>Call for Proposals for the Language Diversity Conference 2026.</strong></p>



<p>To allow more time for submissions and broaden participation, we have <strong>extended the deadline to 15 May 2026</strong>.</p>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Important Dates</h2>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Call for session proposal submission: <strong>Now open</strong> (<a href="https://wikimedia.eventyay.com/wm/langdiv/">submissions via Eventyay</a>)</li>



<li>Call for submissions ends: <strong>15th May 2026</strong></li>



<li>Review of submissions: <strong>Target date 30th May</strong></li>



<li>Notification of acceptance or declination of submissions: <strong>Anticipated to be June</strong></li>
</ul>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading">What’s New</h2>



<p>In response to community feedback and to make the conference more inclusive and accessible, we are introducing <strong>two additional submission formats</strong>:</p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Pre-recorded presentations</strong>: Ideal for contributors who may not be able to attend live due to inability to secure scholarships or self-fund. </li>



<li><strong>Poster presentations</strong>: A flexible format for sharing projects, ideas, research, and community initiatives in a visual and discussion-friendly way.</li>
</ul>



<p>These formats are designed to ensure that more voices can participate meaningfully.</p>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Why the Extension?</h2>



<p>We recognise that participation is shaped by available support, with a limited number of scholarships <strong>(35 international and 19 local)</strong>; not all contributors may be able to self-fund attendance, and we want to ensure everyone has a fair opportunity to prepare and submit strong proposals. Extending the deadline also allows us to reach more communities across regions.</p>



<p>If you’ve been considering submitting, now is the time. We encourage you to take advantage of the extended deadline and the new formats to share your work.</p>



<p>You can submit your proposal in&nbsp;<strong>English, French or Spanish</strong>. The Programming Team will review all submissions after the call closes and will communicate outcomes via email to all applicants.</p>



<p><strong>Note:</strong>&nbsp;By submitting a proposal, you agree that:</p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Your proposal’s abstract and any associated slides or materials will be released under the <a href="https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/3.0/deed.en"><strong>Creative Commons Attribution Share-Alike Licence 4.0</strong></a>; and</li>



<li>If accepted, your session may be <strong>broadcast and/or recorded</strong> and made available in audio and/or visual form under the same licence.</li>
</ul>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Get Involved</h2>

<p>We look forward to your submissions and to building a rich, diverse, and engaging programme together. </p>
