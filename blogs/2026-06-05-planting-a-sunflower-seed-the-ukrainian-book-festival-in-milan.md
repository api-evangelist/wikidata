An Italian Wikimedian organized the first Ukrainian-language edit-a-thon in Italy at a Ukrainian Book Festival in Milan on May 31, 2026, collaborating with Ukrainian colleagues and local Ukrainian associations. The event aimed to build community awareness of Ukrainian Wikipedia and give recognition to a language historically marginalized and suppressed. The initiative represents Wikimedia's broader mission to support linguistic diversity and amplify underrepresented communities through free knowledge.
