Nabunje Leticia shares her experience in Africa Wiki Women's three-month On Wiki Skills Program, where she progressed from learning Wikimedia editing basics to confidently creating Wikipedia articles about notable Ugandan individuals. She also contributed structured data to Wikidata and Wikimedia Commons during the program. The post traces her growth from information consumer to active free-knowledge contributor.
