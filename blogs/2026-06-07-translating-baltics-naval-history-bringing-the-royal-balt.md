This article describes a translation project bringing content about the Royal Baltic Fleet (1658-1856) to Spanish Wikipedia, making important naval history accessible to hundreds of millions of Spanish-speaking readers. The contributor translated and expanded articles to reduce information gaps across linguistic communities. The effort demonstrates how translation work within Wikimedia strengthens the movement's commitment to providing free knowledge across languages.
