Closing the gender gap in knowledge remains a shared priority across the Wikimedia movement. In Nigeria, this commitment came to life through the implementation of WikiGap 2026, led by Wikimedia User Group Nigeria in collaboration with the Embassy of Sweden in Nigeria, and supported by Africa Wiki Women as a strategic partner. WikiGap is a global initiative that seeks to increase the visibility of women on Wikipedia by improving the quantity and quality of content about women.
