The United Kingdom hosted its first national Wiki Science Competition since 2019, with six images advancing to international judging out of all submissions. User Julian Herzog's black-headed gull photograph won overall, followed by entries featuring a sodium laser guide star and a Welsh swan cygnet. The organizer reflects on lessons learned, noting that expanded outreach, campus ambassadors, and social media strategies will be essential for making the 2027 competition more impactful.
