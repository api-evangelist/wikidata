WAMSU Logo with Wikipedia Workshop flyer The Wali Wikimedians Community continues to expand awareness and participation in the Wali Wikipedia Incubator through targeted outreach in tertiary institutions. On 15 January 2026, we organized a Wikipedia workshop for interested students at the University of Business and Integrated Development Studies (UBIDS) in collaboration with the UBIDS Wiki Hub. During this session, participants were introduced to the Waali (Waalɩɩ) orthography, equipping them with foundational knowledge necessary for accurate writing in the language.
